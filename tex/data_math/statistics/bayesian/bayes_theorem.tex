Let $(\Omega, \mathbb{P})$ be a discrete probability space, and let $A, B \subseteq \Omega$ be events with $\mathbb\,{P}(B) > 0$. Then,
\[
\mathbb{P}\,(A|B) = \frac{\mathbb{P}\,(B|A) \cdot \mathbb{P}\,(A)}{\mathbb{P}\,(B)}
\]
\end{wallpaperframe}
