Let $a, b \in \mathbb{Z}$. We say $a$ divides $b$ ($a \mid b$) if there exists $k \in \mathbb{Z}$ such that $b = ak$.
The greatest common divisor of $a$ and $b$ is the largest $d$ such that $d \mid a$ and $d \mid b$.
\end{wallpaperframe}
