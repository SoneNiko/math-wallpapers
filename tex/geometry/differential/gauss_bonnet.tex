\begin{wallpaperframe}
For a compact oriented surface \(M\) with boundary \(\partial M\):
\[
\iint_{M} K \, dA + \oint_{\partial M} \kappa_g \, ds = 2\pi\,\chi(M)
\]
where \(K\) is the Gaussian curvature, \(\kappa_g\) the geodesic curvature,
and \(\chi(M)\) the Euler characteristic.
\end{wallpaperframe}
