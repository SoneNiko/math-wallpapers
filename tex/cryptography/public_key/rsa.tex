\textbf{RSA Encryption} relies on the hardness of integer factorization.
\begin{align*}
  n &= p \cdot q \quad (p,\, q \text{ prime}) \\
  \phi(n) &= (p-1)(q-1) \\
  e \cdot d &\equiv 1 \pmod{\phi(n)} \\
  c &= m^e \bmod n \\
  m &= c^d \bmod n
\end{align*}
