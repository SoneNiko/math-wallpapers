A \textbf{functor} $F : \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D}$ maps objects and morphisms
between categories, preserving composition and identities:
\begin{align*}
  F(\mathrm{id}_A) &= \mathrm{id}_{F(A)} \\
  F(g \circ f)     &= F(g) \circ F(f)
\end{align*}
\end{wallpaperframe}
